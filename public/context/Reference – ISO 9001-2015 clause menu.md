# Reference – ISO 9001:2015 clause menu

This reference is the controlling source for selecting ISO 9001:2015 clauses in the modular prompt stack setup. When this file is provided, use it with priority over the model’s internal memory or general ISO knowledge for clause selection, clause naming, and clause scope.

Use this as a reference menu only. Do not reproduce the full list in the audit plans and similar documents.

Commonly relevant clauses include:

- 4.1 Context of the organisation
- 4.2 Interested parties
- 4.3 Scope of the quality management system
- 4.4 Quality management system and its processes
- 5.1 Leadership and commitment
- 5.2 Quality policy
- 5.3 Organisational roles, responsibilities and authorities
- 6.1 Actions to address risks and opportunities
- 6.2 Quality objectives and planning to achieve them
- 7.1 Resources
- 7.2 Competence
- 7.4 Communication
- 7.5 Documented information
- 8.1 Operational planning and control
- 8.2 Requirements for products and services
- 8.3 Design and development of products and services
- 8.4 Control of externally provided processes, products and services
- 8.5 Production and service provision
- 9.1 Monitoring, measurement, analysis and evaluation
- 9.2 Internal audit
- 9.3 Management review
- 10.2 Nonconformity and corrective action
- 10.3 Continual improvement
